Who We Are

Here at Leaf and Luggage, We are a smol team of four Full Stack Web Developement students enrolled at Atlas Schools located in beautiful downtown Tulsa. Our goal with this project was to show off some of the things we have learned since we began our journey into computer sciences, by creating a web guide on sustainable and eco-friendly travel, accommodations and activities in a few cites across the United States.
