Eco-Friendly Initiatives in Atlanta

Sustainability Facts

🌳 Atlanta has over 48% tree coverage, making it one of the greenest cities in the U.S.

🚴 The BeltLine project promotes sustainable transportation with over 33 miles of multi-use trails.

🌞 Solar energy initiatives are being implemented across the city to reduce carbon footprint.

How to Travel Sustainably in Atlanta

🚶 Walk or bike along the Atlanta BeltLine to reduce emissions.

🚌 Use public transportation like MARTA to get around the city efficiently.

🍽️ Support local and sustainable restaurants that use farm-to-table practices.

🌱 By making eco-friendly choices, you can help Atlanta maintain its green initiatives and support sustainable growth.
